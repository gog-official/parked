Parking Tips

Take a photo of your parking spot with your phone camera. It helps a lot especially in big lots. If you park in a garage always check the level number near the elevator. Set a street cleaning reminder before you leave the car so you never come back to a ticket. Valet mode tracks how long they have your keys so you know exactly how much to pay.

Share your parked location with friends so they can find you without calling five times. The compass feature points you in the right direction even when the map is confusing. All your data stays on your phone nothing gets uploaded anywhere.
